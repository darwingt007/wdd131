Site Plan: Cycling for Health

Color Scheme

My project uses a clean and strong color palette inspired by nature and the sport of cycling. These colors also meet accessibility standards to ensure readability.

  • Primary Color – Deep Forest Blue (#003B4A): Used for the header, footer, section titles, and key navigation elements. This color provides excellent contrast when combined with white text (AAA).
  • Secondary Color – Green (#006400): Used for buttons, highlights, and accents representing health, growth, and the outdoor cycling environment.
  • Background – Light Gray (#F5F5F5): Used for the general page background for a soft, clean appearance.
  • Text – Dark Gray (#333333): Used for body text to maintain strong readability without harsh contrast.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to educate people about how cycling improves health — physically, mentally, and emotionally — and to encourage them to adopt cycling as a long-term wellness habit. The site will offer simple explanations for beginners, highlight the health benefits, and introduce basic cycling performance concepts.

Scenarios

  • How can cycling help improve my physical and emotional health?
  • What type of cycling is best for beginners wanting to improve fitness?
  • How often should I cycle to see health benefits?

Color Schema

The chosen color scheme supports the theme of health (green) and energy/performance (blue).

Primary Green – #006400

Primary Blue – #003B4A

Used for headers, section titles, and emphasis elements.

Light Background – #F5F5F5

Used for the main page background.

Dark Text – #333333

Used for all paragraphs and body text.

Typography

The site will use the following typography:

Font: Arial / Helvetica / Sans-serif

Used for all text on the website for clean readability across devices.
